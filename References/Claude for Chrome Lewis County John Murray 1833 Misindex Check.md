# Claude for Chrome Lewis County John Murray 1833 Misindex Check

This note captures the May 8, 2026 Claude for Chrome follow-up on the last remaining direct early John Murray deed lead in Lewis County. It should be used with [[RQ-M1-JOHN-MURRAY-DEED-FINDINGS|RQ-M1 John Murray Deed Findings]].

## Summary

Claude for Chrome confirmed that the long-carried FamilySearch **1833 John Murray** index entry is another computer-OCR date error, not a pre-1843 deed. The index entry resolves to a fully readable **1899 oil and gas lease** in Lewis County, West Virginia.

**Bottom line:** The supposed **1833** John Murray deed is now a **closed misindex**, just like the earlier Murray / Fish lead. The direct John Murray deed path in RQ-M1 is exhausted.
