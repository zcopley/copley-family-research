# Claude for Chrome Lewis County Murry Vital Records

This note preserves Zach Copley's May 14, 2026 Claude for Chrome verification report for three Lewis County Murry / Murray vital-record targets:

- Luke Murray and Anne Loghan marriage, 1854.
- Patrick Murray and Mary Donahoe / Donahue marriage, 27 December 1862.
- Patrick Murry death, 22 August 1871, Sand Fork of Kent, Lewis County, West Virginia.

**Follow-up status:** A later original-image transcription pass in [[References/Claude for Chrome Lewis County Murry Original Image Transcription]] confirmed the Patrick Murry death-register parent column directly from the WV Culture image. The original manuscript writes Patrick's parents as **Luke & Margaret Murry**.

## Kinship Interpretation

The most important new finding is that the indexed Patrick Murry death entry names **Luke Murry** as Patrick's father and **Margaret Murry** as his mother.

That does **not** prove that the 1854 groom Luke Murray was Patrick's father. If the 1854 Luke was the same man as the 1860 household head born about 1822, he was essentially Patrick's contemporary, not old enough to be Patrick's father. The evidence therefore creates a relationship problem with at least three possibilities:

- The 1871 death-register father was an older Luke Murry, not the 1854 groom.
- The FamilySearch tree birth year for the 1854 Luke is substantially wrong.
- The indexed father field or its interpretation needs correction from the original image.

The later original-image transcription confirms the father field. The safest conclusion is now that Patrick was son of an older **Luke Murry** and **Margaret Murry**, while the exact relationship between Patrick and the 1854 Luke remains unresolved.

## Locality Interpretation

The report places the vital-record trail around the Lewis County courthouse network:

- **Weston** was the county seat where the records were registered.
- **Sheridan** was a northern Lewis County civil district / locality near the West Fork River corridor.
- **Sand Fork of Kent** was a rural southeastern Lewis County locality, roughly 15-20 road miles south-southeast of Weston.
- **St. Michael's Catholic Church** remains the most important parallel sacramental-record lead for these Irish Catholic families.

This places adult Patrick's known 1870-1871 trail in Lewis County, but likely outside the strongest Battelle / Willey farming pages and not yet directly tied to Cove Lick / Camden / Loveberry.
