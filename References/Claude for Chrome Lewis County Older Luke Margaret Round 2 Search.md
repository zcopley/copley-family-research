# Claude for Chrome Lewis County Older Luke Margaret Round 2 Search

This note preserves the follow-up Claude for Chrome search for the older **Luke Murry** and **Margaret Murry** named as Patrick Murry's parents in Patrick's 1871 Lewis County death register.

**Main result:** The Round 2 search did not find an independent online record for the older Luke / Margaret parent couple. It sharpened the negative evidence: the complete indexed 1850 Lewis County Murray / Murry / Murrey population contains **36 people**, all born in Virginia, with **no Luke** and **no Irish-born Murray** in Lewis County.

## 1850 Lewis County Result

The 1850 indexed Lewis County Murray / Murry / Murrey population appears to consist of Virginia-born families, including John Murray, Jane Murray, Hudson Murray, Elizabeth Murray, and associated children or younger people.

No Luke Murray of any age appears in the indexed 1850 Lewis County census, and no Irish-born Murray appears there. Claude reports that Irish-born Murray entries in the 1850 Virginia search appeared in **Preston County**, not Lewis County.

This makes a pre-1850 Lewis County presence for the Ireland-born Luke / Patrick Murry household less likely on the current indexed evidence. The simpler current reading is that the younger Luke Murry and adult Patrick Murry probably arrived in Lewis County after 1850 and before the 1854 Luke Murray / Anne Loghan marriage or before the 1860 census.

## Interpretation

The older Luke Murry named in Patrick's 1871 death register remains real as an informant-reported father, but he still has no independent online record in the searched collections.

The Round 2 negative 1850 result matters because it narrows the likely timeline:

- Patrick Murry and the younger Luke Murry were not found in Lewis County in 1850.
- Both were in the same Lewis County household by 1860.
- The younger Luke married Anne Loghan in Lewis County in 1854.
- Adult Patrick married Mary Donahoe in Lewis County in 1862 and died there in 1871.

The safest working inference is that Patrick and the younger Luke were probably close kin who arrived in Lewis County during the 1850s. If Patrick's father was an older Luke Murry, that older Luke may have died in Ireland, died during / soon after migration, settled elsewhere, or left only non-indexed county / church traces.
