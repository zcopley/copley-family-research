# Genealogical Research Methods

## Historical Context
Copley research spans multiple jurisdictions (Ireland, U.S. federal records, West Virginia county repositories, Catholic parish systems) and mixed evidence quality (primary records, compiled narratives, oral history). A method-first approach is required to avoid conflation, especially with repeated names and variant spellings.

## Copley Family Connection
Method priorities in this project focus on:
- Irish parentage reconstruction for [[Michael Copley Sr]] and [[Ann Copley]].
- Immigration and labor-pathway documentation for early U.S. years.
- Land, lease, and probate economics in [[Places/Lewis County West Virginia|Lewis County, West Virginia]].
- Professional-lineage documentation for [[Michael Joseph Copley]] and [[Stephen Michael Copley]].

## Timeline (Research Program)
- **Phase A**: Assemble known family narrative and disambiguate repeated names.
- **Phase B**: Build evidence matrix by question (Q1, Q3, Q8, Q16, Q24, etc.).
- **Phase C**: Acquire targeted primary records (parish, manifests, deeds, probate).
- **Phase D**: Integrate DNA and documentary findings for kinship confidence scoring.
- **Phase E**: Publish revised narrative with citation tiers and unresolved-gap register.

### DNA Testing Strategies
- Prioritize targeted descendant tests from distinct sibling branches.
- Cluster matches by shared segments and triangulated ancestral couples.
- Use documentary constraints to validate (not replace) DNA inferences.

### Archive Research Techniques
- Build a question-to-record-type matrix before searching.
- Capture full citation metadata (repository, call number, image/page IDs).
- Track negative searches to prevent duplicate effort.

### Record Acquisition Methods
- Courthouse pull list by date range and document class.
- Parish register extraction templates for witnesses/sponsors.
- Manifest surname-variant sweep (Copley/Copely/Cople/Coplee).

## Research Gaps
- Missing primary marriage record for [[Michael Copley Sr]] + [[Ann Copley]].
- No direct payroll proof yet for B&O labor service.
- Incomplete economic reconstruction of oil-lease and inheritance patterns.
- Need broader DNA coverage in collateral lines for robust kinship triangulation.

### Acquisition Strategies
- Sequence acquisition by highest-impact unresolved questions.
- Use a confidence rubric (primary, secondary, oral) on every claim.
- Reconcile contradictions (e.g., date conflicts) with explicit evidence logs.
