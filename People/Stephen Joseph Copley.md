# Stephen Joseph Copley (1970–2023)

📊 View [[Family Tree]] for visual context.

## Biographical Profile

[[Stephen Joseph Copley]] was born **May 1, 1970** at Yale-New Haven Hospital, New Haven, Connecticut — the seventh and youngest child of [[Stephen Michael Copley]] and [[Marcia Thornton Copley]]. Shortly after his birth, the family relocated to Southern California when his father accepted a position at USC.

He was diagnosed early in life as **autistic and severely developmentally disabled**. As he grew older he required constant attention, was nonverbal, and prone to wandering. His care profoundly shaped the entire Copley family and directly influenced the careers and life paths of several of his siblings.

- **Birth:** May 1, 1970, Yale-New Haven Hospital, New Haven, Connecticut
- **Death:** June 10, 2023 (in his sleep, age 53)
- **Burial:** San Juan Bautista, CA, August 8, 2023
- **Residence:** Placed as a client of Harbor Regional Center; cared for at the Intercommunity Exceptional Children's Home, Long Beach, CA; returned home on weekends
- **Legal status:** Became a full-time ward of the State of California (at age 13, when parents divorced in 1983)

His father, [[Stephen Michael Copley]], wrote his biographical sketch in the family history. A Celebration of Life ceremony was held September 7, 2023, at Skylinks Golf Course, Long Beach, CA.

> *"Stephen taught us all a new meaning of love. Through Stephen, we met other young children, individuals with disabilities and their parents, who otherwise we never would have known."* — Stephen Michael Copley
