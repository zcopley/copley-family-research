## Biographical Profile

[[Amy E. Copley Geist|Amy Elizabeth Copley Geist]] is a G26 descendant in the [[Stephen Michael Copley]] line, the only child of Stephen and his second wife [[Judith Ann Todd Copley|Judith Ann Todd Copley]].

- **Full name:** Amy Elizabeth Copley Geist
- **Birth:** July 11, 1990, Hinsdale, Illinois (suburb of Chicago)
- **Parents:** [[Stephen Michael Copley]] and [[Judith Ann Todd Copley]]
- **Spouse:** Nathan ("Nate") Geist — married June 19, 2021, ReFarm Café, State College, PA
- **Current location:** Upper Marlboro, Maryland
- **Occupation:** Analyst, World Bank Africa Gender Innovation Lab (since 2018)
- **Children (G27):** [[William S. Geist]] (William Stephen Geist, b. December 9, 2023)
- **Languages:** French (fluent, learned from French au pairs as child)
