# Sir Richard Copley (c. 1375–1434)

## Biographical Profile

- **Born:** c. 1375, likely Battley (Batley), West Yorkshire, England
- **Died:** 1434
- **Title:** Knight (Sir)
- **Location:** Battley / Battlay, West Yorkshire
- **Significance:** Patriarch of the documented Bredon Copley line; ancestor of Captain John Copley and all subsequent American Copleys descended through that line

## Family Relationships

### Marriages

**First Marriage:** Margaret Denton
- Children: unknown

**Second Marriage:** Elizabeth Harrington
- **Married:** 1423
- **Elizabeth lived:** 1404–1456
- **Children:** Roger Copley (b. 1430)

### Children

- **Roger Copley** (b. 1430) — married Jane Anne Hoo; see [[People/Roger Copley|Roger Copley]]

## Historical Notes

Sir Richard Copley represents the earliest documented generation of the Bredon Copley line. His son Roger married Jane Anne Hoo (b. 1448), establishing the Copley connection to the Hoo family of Kent. Through this marriage, the Copleys gained connection to prominent English families including the Raleighs (the Hoo family was cousins to the Raleigh line through the Newport family).

The second marriage to Elizabeth Harrington in 1423 extended Copley connections to notable Yorkshire and northern English families.

## Research Status

Documentation for Sir Richard Copley at this date is limited. Key sources:

- **Heraldic Visitations:** Sir Richard Copley appears in the Visitations of Yorkshire (recorded genealogical surveys by the College of Arms)
- **Family records:** Continuation through his son Roger documented in Worcestershire and Sussex Visitations
