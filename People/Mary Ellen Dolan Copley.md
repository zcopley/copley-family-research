# Mary Ellen Dolan Copley (1855–1901)

📊 View [[Family Tree]] for visual context.

## Biographical Profile

[[Mary Ellen Dolan Copley]] was born **November 28, 1855** in **Moundsville, West Virginia**, the daughter of **Michael Dolan** (b. 1824, d. 1888) and **Elizabeth Dolan** (b. 1829, d. 1913). She was one of eleven children in the Dolan family. She married [[John Copley]] and settled in [[Places/Lewis County West Virginia|Lewis County, West Virginia]], where she raised five children before her death at age 46.

- **Birth:** November 28, 1855, Moundsville, West Virginia
- **Death:** December 5, 1901 (age 46)
- **Parents:** Michael Dolan (1824–1888) and Elizabeth Dolan (1829–1913)
- **Marriage:** [[John Copley]]
- **Children:** 5 (Thomas E., Mary, Anne, Nelle, Michael Joseph)
