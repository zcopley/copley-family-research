# Susan Copley (b. 1967)

📊 View [[Family Tree]] for visual context.

## Biographical Profile

[[Susan Copley]] is a G26 descendant in the [[Stephen Michael Copley]] line — the sixth of seven children from Stephen's first marriage to [[Marcia Thornton Copley]].

- **Birth:** 1967
- **Birth context:** likely during the family's [[Places/Madison Connecticut|Madison, Connecticut]] years, before the 1970 move to California
- **Parents:** [[Stephen Michael Copley]] and [[Marcia Thornton Copley]]

## Biographical Narrative

Susan belongs to the younger half of Stephen and Marcia's first-marriage household: the children born after the early Berkeley and graduate-school years, during the more settled [[Places/Madison Connecticut|Madison, Connecticut]] stage that preceded the family's 1970 move to [[Places/Palos Verdes Estates California|Palos Verdes Estates]]. That placement matters because the appendix evidence for her is mostly family-context material rather than a standalone first-person sketch.

The strongest direct detail comes from [[Stephen Michael Copley|her father]]'s recollections of California family life. In his appendix sketch, Stephen remembers coaching Susan's soccer team and both Susan's and [[Sara Copley Cox|Sara's]] softball teams during the Palos Verdes years. That places Susan inside one of the clearest recurring themes of the later Stephen-line household: organized sports, active parenting, and a California family culture built around school, church, beaches, patio barbecues, Disneyland outings, and Sierra camping trips.

Because Susan's page rests on thinner evidence than some of her siblings' pages, it should be read mainly as a branch-context profile rather than a fully documented adult biography. Even so, the available appendix material places her clearly within the same Madison-to-Palos-Verdes transition that shaped [[Philip Copley]], [[Peter Copley]], and the rest of the younger sibling cohort.
