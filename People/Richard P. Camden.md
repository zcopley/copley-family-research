# Richard P. Camden

## Biographical Profile

[[Richard P. Camden]] appears in Appendix 2 of the family history as one of the Camden, Bailey and Camden land speculators tied to the proposed Copley farm origin chain. The appendix describes him as a businessman and [[People/Weeden Hoffman|Weeden Hoffman]]'s partner in the first general store in Weston.

## Land-Chain Role

Appendix 2 proposes that Hoffman may have obtained the land warrant behind his December 30, 1842 Commonwealth of Virginia grant through Camden, Bailey and Camden, possibly through his business relationship with Richard P. Camden. That makes Richard the most direct possible bridge between Hoffman's local business network and the speculative land claims that preceded the Copley purchase.
