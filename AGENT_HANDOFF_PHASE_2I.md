# Phase 2I Handoff: 1634 Visitation of Worcestershire Analysis

## Overview

The 1634 Visitation of Worcestershire PDF (pp. 26–27) has been converted to text via `pdftotext` and fully analyzed. This primary source document contains the complete pedigree of the Copley family of Norton in Bredon, Worcestershire, and provides significant new detail about the pre-G23 English ancestor line.

**Key outcome:** One major AI hallucination corrected (Captain John's wife), plus rich new documentation of the Thomas Copley Sr. family structure.

---

## What Changed

### Major Correction: Captain John Copley's Wife

**Before (speculative):** His wife was named **Jane** in AI-generated narratives (possibly Jane Lode or Jane Loftus), but no primary source confirmed her identity.

**Now (verified):** The **1634 Visitation of Worcestershire** (p. 26) directly records his wife as **Margaret, daughter of George Newport of Rushock**.

**Status:** ✅ VERIFIED primary source. All references updated.

**Impact:** Updated in `Topics/Captain John Copley Research.md` (Generation 3 section, Descent Diagram, Sources), and new person page created.

### Thomas Copley Sr.'s Family Structure (EXPANDED)

✅ **[VERIFIED — 1634 Visitation]** Thomas Copley Sr. (d. May 20, 1593) had at least **10 documented children**:

**Sons (numbered 1–5 in the pedigree):**
1. **Thomas Copley Jr.** (present at 1634 Visitation; d. 1647; manor sold 1649)
2. **Captain John Copley** ("in Italy" 1634; c. 1610–post 1657)
3. **William Copley of London** (1634)
4. **Nicholas Copley of London** (1634)
5. **Arthur Copley** (1634)

**Daughters:**
- Elizabeth (m. John Parsons of Kemerton, Glos.)
- Mary (m. John Skipp of Ledbury, Heref.)
- Susan (m. Leonard Jeffrey of Earl's Croome, mar.lic. 1582 Wore.)
- Margaret, Katherine, Jane, Hester, Anne, Bridget

**Also in this generation:** William Copley (b. 1574, d. 1611), Barrister-at-law at Middle Temple — likely a son of Thomas Sr.

### Thomas Copley Jr.'s Marriages and Children (NEW DETAIL)

✅ **[VERIFIED — 1634 Visitation, pp. 26–27]** Thomas Jr. had **two wives**:
- **Wife 1:** Margaret, daughter of Thomas Hanford of Wollashull
- **Wife 2:** Frances, daughter of Sir George Browne (2nd son of Viscount Montagu)

**Children:**
- Thomas (b. c. 1627, aged ~7 in 1634)
- George
