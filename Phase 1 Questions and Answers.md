# Phase 1 Questions and Answers

This page addresses all **Phase 1 quick-win questions** from the strategy analysis and documents what is known, unknown, and the next action.

📊 Use [[Family Tree]] as the visual reference when mapping each research question to specific people and branches.

## Q23 — Who was the “Tom” at Chancellorsville?

### Current answer
Family tradition references a “Tom” who fought at Chancellorsville, but this appears to be an **identity mismatch** in the current compiled narrative. No reliable documentary chain yet links the story to a clearly identified person in this line (including [[Thomas E. Copley]]).

### Gap analysis
- **Known:** A family memory exists; narrative inconsistency has been flagged.
- **Unknown:** Which Thomas (if any) served; exact relationship to core line.

### Strategy to close gap
- Build collateral-line matrix of all Thomas/Tom Copleys born c.1820–1850.
- Search Civil War CMSR/pension indexes and unit rosters.
- Reconcile with family tree timelines to eliminate impossible candidates.

### Citations
- Internal analysis note (Q23): file-based synthesis from family records.
- NARA military records gateway: <https://www.archives.gov/research/military/civil-war>
