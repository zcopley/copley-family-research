# Weston, West Virginia

## Geographic Information
- 🗺️ **Google Maps:** [View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Weston+West+Virginia)
- **Coordinates:** 39.0418, -80.4673
- **Current administration:** City of Weston, Lewis County seat, West Virginia.
- **Historical names/boundaries:** records may appear under Lewis County, Virginia (pre-1863) and later West Virginia.

## Historical Context (1800s-1900s)
As county seat, Weston concentrated legal, church, and market functions for surrounding farm communities. This made it a key records hub for marriage, probate, and land evidence.

- **Economy:** county-seat services + surrounding agricultural production.
- **Administrative role:** deed/probate/marriage archive center.
- **Church context:** Catholic records in/near Weston are significant to Copley research.

## Copley Family Connection
- Birth-area context for [[John Copley]] (1843, near Weston).
- Core record locus for [[Michael Copley Sr]] and [[Ann Copley]] family events.
- Strong connection to farm continuity and oil-era legal documentation.
