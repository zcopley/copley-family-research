# Palos Verdes Estates, California

## Geographic Information
- 🗺️ **Google Maps:** [View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Palos+Verdes+Estates+California)
- **Approx. coordinates:** 33.7878, -118.4011
- **Current administration:** Palos Verdes Estates, Los Angeles County, California.
- **Historical names/boundaries:** affluent South Bay residential community on the Palos Verdes Peninsula.

## Historical Context (1900s)
In this project, Palos Verdes Estates is the long Southern California phase of the Stephen line. It is where university life, child-rearing, music, and academic entertaining all converge during Stephen Copley's USC years.

- **Professional context:** University of Southern California faculty and department-leadership period.
- **Household context:** family home at **4029 Via Nivel**.
- **Branch role:** major setting for the G26 Stephen-line childhood years.

## Copley Family Connection
- [[Stephen Michael Copley]] lived at **4029 Via Nivel** in Palos Verdes Estates from roughly `1970-1990` while teaching and leading in materials science at USC.
- The family appendix presents the Palos Verdes years as the central Southern California childhood setting for [[Michael Copley (b. 1959)]], [[Philip Copley]], [[Peter Copley]], [[Susan Copley]], and their siblings.
- [[Peter Copley]]'s sketch highlights local schools, music, soccer, family gatherings, and a family-supported band project in the Palos Verdes years.
