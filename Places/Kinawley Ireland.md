# Kinawley, Ireland

## Geographic Information
- 🗺️ **Google Maps:** [View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Kinawley+Ireland)
- **Approx. coordinates:** 54.26, -7.45
- **Current administration:** County Fermanagh area (Northern Ireland), near County Cavan boundary.
- **Historical names/boundaries:** records can appear under Kinawley civil/parish forms with cross-border administrative complexity.

## Historical Context (1800s-1900s)
Kinawley was part of a rural borderland economy with heavy dependence on small farming. Family movement from this area into transatlantic migration streams was common in the early 19th century.

- **Economy:** agricultural and parish-centered community life.
- **Migration pattern:** family-based emigration to U.S. river/rail corridors.
- **Record challenge:** scattered Catholic and civil archive pathways across jurisdictions.

## Copley Family Connection
- Reported birthplace of [[Ann Copley]] (Ann Elizabeth Munday tradition), born 1823.
- Associated with family tradition that Ann’s father died in the Potomac after migration.
- Critical origin node for verifying the Munday line.

## Research Resources
- PRONI (Public Record Office of Northern Ireland): parish/civil holdings.
- NLI registers portal (cross-checking Catholic records): <https://registers.nli.ie>
- Local Fermanagh genealogy societies and parish transcription projects.
- Newspaper databases for surname variants tied to migration narratives.
