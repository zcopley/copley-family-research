# Baltimore, Maryland

## Geographic Information
- 🗺️ **Google Maps:** [View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Baltimore+Maryland)
- **Coordinates:** 39.2904, -76.6122
- **Current administration:** Independent City of Baltimore, Maryland, USA.
- **Historical names/boundaries:** “Baltimore City” and Archdiocese-based parish references are common in 19th-century records.

## Historical Context (1800s-1900s)
Baltimore was a major immigrant port and the strategic hub of the B&O Railroad system. Irish migrants moving into rail labor and western settlement corridors commonly passed through or worked from Baltimore-linked networks.

- **Economy:** port commerce, rail administration, manufacturing.
- **Immigration pattern:** strong Irish migration and labor recruitment base.
- **Record significance:** rich Catholic and civil archival systems.
