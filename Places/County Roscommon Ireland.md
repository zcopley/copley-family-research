# County Roscommon, Ireland

## Geographic Information

- **Historical names/boundaries:** records distributed across parish, barony, and estate systems.

## Historical Context (1800s-1900s)
Roscommon was a core agrarian county with high tenancy pressure and persistent emigration. For genealogy, county-level context is essential because families could appear across neighboring parishes and spellings.

- **Economy:** agriculture/tenancy with limited industrial absorption.
- **Migration pattern:** sustained movement to U.S. labor and settlement fronts.
- **Research implication:** triangulate church, land, tax, and estate records.
