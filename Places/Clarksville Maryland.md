# Clarksville, Maryland

## Geographic Information
- 🗺️ **Google Maps:** [View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Clarksville+Maryland)
- **Approx. coordinates:** 39.2126, -76.9450
- **Current administration:** Clarksville, Howard County, Maryland.
- **Historical names/boundaries:** described in the family appendix as a rural Maryland town during the Sardo family's early years there.

## Historical Context (1900s)
Within this project, Clarksville matters as the first settled Maryland home of the Sardo branch after Nelle's nursing formation in Baltimore. It represents the move from hospital training and urban professional life into a family-centered medical household.

- **Branch context:** early Sardo family home.
- **Religious context:** proximity to **St. Louis Catholic Church**.
- **Professional context:** Robert Sardo's local medical practice.

## Copley Family Connection
- [[Ellen Bernadine Nelle Copley Sardo]] and Robert Samuel Sardo lived in Clarksville after their `1931` marriage.
- The family appendix says Robert began his practice there from their home across from **St. Louis Catholic Church**.
- [[Sarah Ellen Sardo Arena]]'s sketch treats Clarksville as the rural setting of her earliest childhood before the family's World War II move into Baltimore.
