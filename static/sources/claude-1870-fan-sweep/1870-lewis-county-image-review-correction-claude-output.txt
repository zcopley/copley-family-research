New / corrected Ireland-born surnames now identified on image 8, Battelle:

Cluster geography: The Battelle Irish cluster on image 8 now shows four Ireland-born households in sequence: Bush (HH 19), Leely (HH 21), Mulooly (HH 22), McDonald (HH 23). All four are farmers. This is a dense rural Irish settlement within the Battelle district, all enumerated by the same enumerator on August 23, 1870. No Murray / Murry household appears among them.

Image 265, Willey: No new Ireland-born surname was found. The area before Cummings was occupied by the Virginia-born Butcher household. The Willey district Irish cluster remains as documented: Hanrahan (image 261) and Cummings (image 265).
