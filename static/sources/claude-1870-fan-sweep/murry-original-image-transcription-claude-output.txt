Summary of access problems:
Luke Murray county marriage Q2ZX-G78G: FamilySearch Image Unavailable; WV Culture not available.
Luke Murray bond FRRG-HPT: FamilySearch thumbnail only, unreadable; WV Culture blank frames at Film 825111 Images 183-185.
Patrick Murray marriage Q2ZF-7BF6: FamilySearch image readable, Image 652.
Patrick Murry death NMD7-M2L: FamilySearch abstract only; WV Culture full register image readable at Film 825106 Image 307.

Conclusion:
The original death image really names Luke Murry as Patrick's father. The death register entry, WV Culture Film 825106, Image 307, page 69, line 19, unambiguously writes the parents as Luke & Margaret Murry in the Parents' Names column. This is not a secondary abstraction or a FamilySearch transcription error.

The original death record gives no age for the father, no further identification, and no additional detail beyond the name pair Luke & Margaret Murry and Patrick's birthplace of Ireland.

The 1854 Luke Murray is almost certainly too young to be Patrick's father if his approximate 1822 birth year is correct. A man born about 1822-1826 would have been only about 1-3 years old when Patrick was born about 1823. The death record's Luke is therefore most likely a separate, older Luke Murry, born about 1790-1800 in Ireland, who was Patrick's father and distinct from the 1854 bridegroom. No direct primary evidence for this older Luke was found within these four records.
