Rejected candidates:

Luke Murray 1822-1899, KHHZ-4BV, married Anne Loghan 30 Dec 1854, Lewis County. Rejected as Patrick's father because he was born about 1822 in Ireland, only about one year older than Patrick born about 1823. The 1860 census shows he and Patrick, age 34, living in the same Lewis County household, consistent with brothers or cousins, not father and son.

Margaret Murray, died 3 Dec 1880, Lewis County, WV Culture Id 2920212, age 63, Ireland. Rejected because her parents were J. Collins and B. Collins, maiden name Collins; spouse listed as J. Murray, not Luke.

Ellen Murray, died 22 Apr 1928, Lewis County, WV Culture Id 412833. Rejected because parents are listed as Ann Lohan, mother, and Patrick White, father. Mother Ann Lohan identifies her as connected to younger Luke Murray who married Anne Loghan, or another distinct family issue, not Patrick Murry's daughter Ellen.

Margaret Murray, died 19 Jun 1928, Lewis County, WV Culture Id 1908819. Rejected because parents are both unknown and spouse is W.J. Murray; cannot be connected to either Luke without further evidence.

Collateral clues from Patrick's widow and children:

1860 Census, Lewis, Virginia:
Patrick Murry, age 34, Ireland, lived in the same household as younger Luke Murry, age 38, Ireland, married to Ann Murry age 24, with children Patrick 4, John 2, Mary 1, and Owen Loghan 11, Virginia. Patrick was unmarried in 1860. This is the last record showing Patrick before his 1862 marriage and 1871 death.

1862 Marriage, Lewis County:
Patrick Murray married Mary Donahoe on 27 December 1862, Lewis County, WV. The marriage record does not name parents of either party.

1871 Death Register:
Patrick Murry, died 22 Aug 1871, age 48, Ireland, farmer, Sand Fork of Kent, Lewis County. Parents named as Luke & Margaret Murry. Informant: Mary S. Murry, wife. This is the sole primary record naming his parents.

1880 Census, Weston, Lewis, WV:
Mary Murray, head, age 42, Ireland, with children Mary A. Donahoe 21 WVA, Martin J. Donahoe 18 WVA, Ellen Murray 17 WVA, Luke Murray 9 WVA. Father's birthplace Ireland; mother's birthplace Ireland. No grandparents named. Patrick's son Luke was born 17 Sep 1869 per his 1955 death certificate.

Luke Murray death, 2 Jun 1955, Clarksburg, Harrison County, WV, WV Culture Id 547408:
Luke Murray, age 85, born 17 Sep 1869, Weston, WV. Parents Patrick Murray, deceased, and Mary Turner, deceased. Mary Turner is almost certainly a transcription error for Mary Donahoe / Murray. Buried Holy Cross Cemetery, Clarksburg. Confirms Patrick as father; no grandparents named.

No death records found for Mary A. Donahoe, Martin J. Donahoe, or the known daughter Margaret Murray that name grandparents Luke & Margaret.

Conclusion:

No direct evidence for older Luke & Margaret surfaced in accessible digitized and indexed records. Within WV Culture vital records, FamilySearch census, death, marriage and birth collections, and Find A Grave, there is no independent record of an older Luke Murry / Murray, Patrick's father, or his wife Margaret. Every Luke Murray found in Lewis County records is either the younger Luke born 1822, married Anne Loghan 1854, or Patrick's son Luke born 1869, died 1955 in Clarksburg. No record places an older Luke born circa 1780-1805 in Ireland in Lewis, Gilmer, or Harrison County.

The evidence clearly separates the older Luke from the 1854 Luke. The 1860 census shows the 1854 Luke, age 38, born 1822, and Patrick, age 34, born about 1826 in that entry, as contemporaries living in the same Lewis County household. A man born 1822 could not be father of a man born about 1823. They are almost certainly brothers or first cousins, not father and son.

The most probable explanation for the absence of an older Luke record is that Patrick's father Luke died in Ireland or before West Virginia formal record keeping began. Alternatively, he may have died in the 1840s or early 1850s before emigrating, or died in Lewis / Gilmer County before registration commenced. The 1871 death register captures what Mary S. Murry, Patrick's widow, reported about her deceased father-in-law.
