Interpretation:

The records do not support Luke and the adult Patrick Murry who died in 1871 as brothers without qualification. The death register index names Luke Murry as Patrick's father and Margaret Murry as his mother. If that father is the same Luke Murray who married Anne Loghan in 1854 and is born about 1822 in the FamilySearch tree, the dates are impossible because Patrick was born about 1823. This suggests at least one unresolved issue: the Luke named as father may be an older Luke Murry; the FamilySearch tree birth year for the 1854 Luke may be wrong; there may be two different Luke Murrys; or the indexed father field must be checked against the original image. Original death-register image verification is required before drawing a final kinship conclusion.
